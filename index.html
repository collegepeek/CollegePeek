PeekIn to your future


Finding the right college is hard,
especially for international students.


Choosing the right college is in essence choosing the 4 very important years of your life, which will greatly shape you as a person.
At CollegePeek, our ambassadors from various colleges are dedicatd to help you peek-in to the actual college life, before you apply or matriculate, to help you make the best decision possible.


We offer a simple 30 minutes Skype session that you will take full advantage of by clarifying all your doubts and concerns with our on-site ambassadors. Get a head start today by connecting with our college student ambassadors to better understand your options and college application.

How to PeekIn


Connect

Schedule a 30-minute free session with any of our ambassadors and chat away!

Ask

Ask about their dorms, social life, atheletics, food, city setting and diversity - what the real life on campus is like

Aha!

Imagine yourself standing on that campus - have you found where you would like to be? If not, PeekIn to others to discover more!

Match

Find the one (or few) colleges that fit your vision for the next 4 years

Team

We are a team of enthusiastic student entrepreneurs who wanted to provide an easy and practical solution to the problem that we all went through just a couple of years back.

We founded CollegePeek because for us, it's about the people, atmosphere, opportunities, and everything else that comes in between. We want to help you find out about what the real college life will look like, in much simpler and more approachable way!
